What is the difference between Rival 700 and Rival 710?

With the Rival 710, we’ve updated the sensor from the 3360 to our latest TrueMove3 custom sensor. We’ve also updated the switches to industry-leading SteelSeries 60 million click mechanical switches.

TrueMove3? What about more CPI?

The usable range of CPI in the TrueMove3 is more than necessary for the current environment, and as we know that CPI is not a measure of true performance. A sensor going up to 350+ IPS is exceptional, and we don’t expect users to be maxing that out.
